Nasi Goreng

Description

Nasi Goreng is a beloved Indonesian fried rice dish known for its savory, slightly sweet, and smoky flavor. It's often served with a fried egg on top and garnished with cucumber or tomato.

This version is quick, delicious, and can be made with leftover rice and simple pantry ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 shallot or onion, chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chicken, shrimp, or vegetables

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add any optional protein or vegetables and cook until done.
  4. Push ingredients aside and scramble the egg in the pan.
  5. Add the rice and stir well to combine everything.
  6. Drizzle with sweet soy sauce, stir-fry for a few more minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste, serve hot with a fried egg on top.
